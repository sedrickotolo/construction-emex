Who we are

EECL is majorly involved in all types of Industrial Training which is essential for Oil & gas sector like HSE, Industrial Non-Destructive, Welding & Painting inspections. This would be a big opportunity for fresh & amateur engineers in country to develop their skills.

EECL also provides industrial, marine and commercial sandblasting and painting services. We render such services, as refurbishment for oil and gas storage tanks for both above ground as well as buried tanks, maintenance of commercial and industrial facilities which complies to the all-International standard specifications. The company also arranges marine and offshore rope access services. We utilize industrial air-less spray & conventional applications with having expertise in heavy duty Marine and Protective paints & coatings such as Epoxies, Polyurethanes, Acrylics, Alkyds, Sealants and Synthetics.

We also dealing with the sales of some specialized equipment’s in industrial painting, inspection tools, NDT inspection etc.
